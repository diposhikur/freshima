Emerging

Master algebra, geometry, and foundational math skills to excel in your freshman year.

About This Course

This course is designed for freshman students to build a strong foundation in mathematics. Covering key topics like algebra, geometry, and basic calculus, it prepares you for academic success in science, engineering, and beyond.

What You'll Learn

  • Solve linear and quadratic equations with confidence.
  • Understand geometric shapes, theorems, and proofs.
  • Apply basic calculus concepts to real-world problems.
  • Develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

Course Content

Lesson 1: Introduction to Algebra

Learn the basics of variables, equations, and algebraic expressions.

Lesson 2: Geometry Fundamentals

Explore shapes, angles, and theorems in geometry.

Lesson 3: Introduction to Calculus

Understand limits, derivatives, and their applications.
