About This Course

This course is designed for freshmen students to build a strong foundation in anthropology. Covering key topics like cultural anthropology, biological anthropology, and archaeology, it prepares you for academic success in social sciences and beyond.

What You'll Learn

  • Understand the diversity of human cultures and societies.
  • Explore human evolution and biological adaptations.
  • Analyze archaeological methods and findings.
  • Develop critical thinking and cross-cultural analysis skills.
