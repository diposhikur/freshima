Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of FreshLearn courses?
FreshLearn courses are premium offerings with pricing based on course content and duration. Check our pricing page for details.
How do I enroll in a course?
Simply register for an account, browse our courses, and click "Start Learning" to enroll.
Can I access courses on mobile?
Yes, our platform is fully responsive and accessible on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.
What types of courses are available?
We offer courses in Maths, English, Physics, Geography, Psychology, and more, tailored for freshman students.
Do I get a certificate after completing a course?
Yes, you receive a digital certificate of completion for each course you successfully finish.
How long do I have access to a course?
Once enrolled, you have lifetime access to the course materials, including updates.
Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you’re not satisfied with your course purchase.
Is there support available for course content?
Yes, our support team and instructors are available via email and discussion forums to assist you.
